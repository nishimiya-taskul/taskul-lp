## 英語テンプレート

### Short Description (150 chars)
```
AI-powered task management for creative freelancers. Paste client messages, AI structures projects, tasks, estimates & invoices automatically.
```

### Product Description (500 chars)
```
TASKUL is an AI-native project management tool built specifically for creative freelancers and small teams. Simply paste your client's Slack messages, emails, or meeting notes — our Director AI automatically structures everything into projects, tasks, requirements, and even draft estimates. Features include AI project structuring, automated estimates & invoices, Google Calendar sync, LINE notifications, Slack integration, partner management with AI team analysis, and a built-in AI sales advisor. Designed by creators who've delivered 3,000+ web projects.
```

### Long Description
```
TASKUL is the first AI-native task management platform designed exclusively for creative professionals — web designers, developers, video creators, and freelance directors.

The Problem:
Creative freelancers juggle multiple clients, deadlines, and deliverables using scattered spreadsheets, generic PM tools, and manual invoicing. Context gets lost, deadlines slip, and admin work eats into creative time.

The Solution:
Paste any client conversation (Slack, email, meeting notes) into TASKUL. Our Director AI — trained on thousands of real creative projects — automatically extracts:
• Project scope & requirements
• Task breakdown with deadlines
• Risk factors & client expectations
• Draft estimates with line items

Key Features:
• AI Project Structuring — paste text, get a complete project
• AI Estimates & Invoices — one-click generation from project data
• Slack Integration — /taskul command creates projects from conversations
• Google Calendar Sync — tasks and meetings in one view
• LINE Notifications — daily task reminders via LINE
• Partner Management — track freelancer skills, rates, availability
• AI Team Analysis — radar chart of team capabilities
• AI Sales Advisor — negotiation coaching for freelancers
• Smart Dashboard — today's and this week's deadlines at a glance

Built by creators, for creators. 150+ creative professionals participated in development.
```
